Actions

Every action makes a change in the state of your running trip.

#Add to call

Add a player to a conference call.

FieldTypeDescription
Role nameRoleThe role to add to the call.

#Adjust page

Send an ephemeral signal to a player. This is deprecated.

FieldTypeDescription
Role nameRoleThe role to send the adjustment to.
New valuestringThe adjustment value to send.

#Conditional

A conditional branch.

FieldTypeDescription
IfCondition
Actionslist
Actions → ItemsAction
Elseifslist
Elseifs → Itemsobject
Elseifs → Items → IfCondition
Elseifs → Items → Actionslist
Elseifs → Items → Actions → ItemsAction
Elselist
Else → ItemsAction

#Increment value

Increment the numerical value of a value by reference.

FieldTypeDescription
Value refsimpleAttribute
Deltanumber

#Initiate call

Initiate a call from one player to another.

FieldTypeDescription
To role nameRoleThe player to initiate a call to.
As role nameRoleThe player from whom the call originates.

#Pause audio

Pause currently playing audio.

FieldTypeDescription
Role nameRoleThe role to pause the audio for.

#Play audio

Start playing audio for a certain role.

FieldTypeDescription
Role nameRoleThe role to play the audio for.

#Play clip

Play a clip on an active phone call.

FieldTypeDescription
Clip nameClipThe clip to play.

#Resume audio

Resume currently paused audio.

FieldTypeDescription
Role nameRoleThe role to resume the audio for.

#Send audio

Send an audio message from one player to another.

FieldTypeDescription
From role nameRoleThe role to send the message from.
To role nameRoleThe role to send the message to.

#Send email

Send an email from one player to another.

FieldTypeDescription
FromInboxInbox to send from.
ToRoleRole to send to.
SubjectstringSubject line for the email.
BodymarkdownBody of the email.
CcemailEmail address to CC.
BccemailEmail address to BCC.

#Send image

Send an image from one player to another.

FieldTypeDescription
From role nameRoleThe role to send the message from.
To role nameRoleThe role to send the message to.

#Send text

Send a text message from one player to another.

FieldTypeDescription
From role nameRoleThe role to send the message from.
To role nameRoleThe role to send the message to.
ContentstringThe content of the message to send.

#Send to page

Set a player to a page by role.

FieldTypeDescription
Role nameRoleThe role to send to a page.
Page namePageThe page to send the matching players to.

#Set value

Update a value in the trip state to either a constant number or string, or to match another value by reference.

FieldTypeDescription
Value refsimpleAttribute
New value reflookupable

#Signal cue

Signal a cue. A cue does nothing on its own, but usually will have triggers attached, which fire actions.

FieldTypeDescription
Cue nameCueThe cue to signal.

#Start scene

Start a new scene.

FieldTypeDescription
Scene nameScene

#Stop audio

Stop audio and reset audio state.

FieldTypeDescription
Role nameRoleThe role to stop the audio for.

#Wait

Wait a fixed period of time.

FieldTypeDescription
DurationtimeOffsetA duration, i.e. 1h, 30s, 4.2m

#Wait before time

Wait a fixed period of time before a scheduled time.

FieldTypeDescription
UntilTime
BeforetimeOffsetA time offset, i.e. 1h, 30s, 4.2m

#Wait for time

Wait a fixed period of time.

FieldTypeDescription
UntilTime
